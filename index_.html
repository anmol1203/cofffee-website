about us

what makes our coffee special?

The flavors hidden in a cup of coffee are rich and crisp, and this comes not just from the quality of the specialty coffee beans, but from the work ethic and dedication put into it by the farmer.

The medium-roast coffee beans ooze flavors of decadent milk chocolate balanced against bright, sweet cherries, canopied by a slight caramel undertone. Make sure to try this and more exquisite flavors available on our website or at a CAFENA near you.
